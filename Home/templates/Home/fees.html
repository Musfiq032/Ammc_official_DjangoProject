For Local Students

In line with the DGHS circular on the expense for MBBS course, our monthly tuition fee has been fixed at Tk. 8,000/= (BDT Eight Thousand only). Tuition fees for the first two months shall be payable at the time of admission. Subsequently tuition fees are payable on a monthly basis, however anyone can also pay in advance for any number of months. Total amount of one time admission (non-refundable) and other fees for depositing during the admission time will be fixed and announced shortly, to be circulated in our website and separate publication as per government circular & competent authority.
