A Few Words
from the president of DAM

Dhaka Ahsania Mission (DAM), a renowned Non-Government Organization of Bangladesh had been serving the nation in different humanitarian sectors like health, micro-credit and education for more than 60 years with a proven success story. With a view to diversify its further activities, DAM has recently extended the services of AMCGH by establishing its educational wing – Ahsania Mission Medical College (AMMC) to impart medical education to the future generation by maintaining highest level of standard.
