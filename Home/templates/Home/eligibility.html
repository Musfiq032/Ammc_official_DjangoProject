Eligibility

  1. The applicant shall be a Bangladeshi Citizen.
  2. He/ She must have passed HSC or equivalent examination in 2021 or 2022 with Physics, Chemistry and Biology.
  3. They must have the minimum standard pass mark set by the concerned government authority at the last combined MBBS Admission Test held on 2022.
  4. As menioned above, students must qualify the MBBS admission test conducted by the Director General of Health Services (DGHS).
  5. A level examination passed candidates must submit equivalent certificate from Directorate General of Health Services, Govt. of Bangladesh along with the application.

Admission shall be on merit basis and follow the same criteria as prescribed by the Ministry of Health & Family Welfare and Bangladesh Medical & Dental Council. Applications are invited for admission to first year MBBS class (First Batch) for the session 2021-2022.
