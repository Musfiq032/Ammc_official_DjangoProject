  • Admission Guidlines

Applications are to be submitted in prescribed forms obtainable from the college office. Students interested to apply for admission in MBBS Course at AMMC will have to collect hard copy of the admission form by depositing Tk. 1000/= (BDT One Thousand only) from the college premises. Thereafter while submission of the filled in admission form the students will have to submit the following documents also.


  1. Money receipt of application fee.
  2. Photocopy of S.S.C certificate & mark sheet/ equivalent examination certificate & mark sheet.
  3. Photocopy of H.S.C certificate & mark sheet/ equivalent examination certificate & mark sheet.
  4. Photocopy of Testimonial from School Head Master.
  5. Photocopy of National Identity Card (NID) / Birth Certificate.
  6. 4 passport size & 3 stamp size recent color photographs.
  7. Certificate of character from a Class I Gazetted Officer.
  8. Certificate regarding financial solvency of Parents/Guardian.
  9. Original copies of above documents are to be produced at the time of admission.
