Administration

As per the instruction of the Ministry of Health & Family Welfare and Dhaka University, a 15 member Governing Body has been formed with its Chairman Mr. Kazi Rafiqul Alam, also the President of DAM & Chairman of AMCGH Governing Body. This committee also included representatives from Ministry of Health & Family Welfare, Dhaka University and other affiliated bodies.

The Principal of AMMC has been tipped as the Member Secretary of the Governing Body.

The regular administrative set up will be under the direct supervision of the Principal along with other full time employees of AMMC, AMCGH & DAM.
